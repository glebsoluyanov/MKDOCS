# 123 ARROWS, MYSTIC WEAPONS, BOWS, POLE ARMS

## Crafting Dual Swords

Blacksmiths will craft several of the dual swords for you, if you supply them with the original swords and sufficient adena. See the Craft column for their service charge, in adena.

## ARROWS

Note that arrows can only be used with bows of equal grade. For example, Bone Arrows can only be used with D-grade bows.

| WEIGHT | PAYS | GRADE |
|--------|------|-------|
| Wooden Arrow | 6 | 1 | N |
| Bone Arrow | 5 | 1 | D |
| Fine Steel Arrow | 4 | 3 | C |
| Silver Arrow | 4 | 4 | B |
